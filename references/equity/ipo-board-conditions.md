## 3. 科创板 (STAR Board) — Financial Standards

### Innovation Attributes Check (定性+定量要求) — ALL applicants must pass

| Check Item | Threshold |
|---|---|
| R&D investment / Revenue | ≥ 5% (recent 3 years cumulative) **OR** R&D expenditure ≥ RMB 60 million (recent 3 years cumulative) |
| Patents (invention patents forming core revenue) | ≥ 5 |
| Revenue CAGR (recent 3 years) | ≥ 20% **OR** most recent year revenue ≥ RMB 3 billion |
| *Additional qualitative indicators* | National S&T awards, provincial-level major projects, import substitution, etc. |

### Standard 1: Market Cap + Profit
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 1 billion |
| Net profit (most recent 2 years, both positive) | Cumulative ≥ RMB 50 million |

### Standard 2: Market Cap + Revenue + R&D
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 1.5 billion |
| Revenue (most recent year) | ≥ RMB 200 million |
| R&D investment / Revenue (recent 3 years) | ≥ 15% |

### Standard 3: Market Cap + Revenue + Cash Flow
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 2 billion |
| Revenue (most recent year) | ≥ RMB 300 million |
| Operating cash flow (recent 3 years cumulative) | ≥ RMB 100 million |

### Standard 4: Market Cap + Revenue
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 3 billion |
| Revenue (most recent year) | ≥ RMB 300 million |

### Standard 5: Market Cap Only (Pre-revenue)
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 4 billion |
| Core technology achieves breakthrough or belongs to key national strategic area |
| At least one core product approved for commercial use |
| Clear path to large-scale revenue |

### STAR Board — VIE / Red-Chip / Dual-Class Share Requirements

| Type | Market Cap Requirement | Additional Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| **Red-chip (already listed overseas)** | ≥ RMB 200 billion | + leading market position & strong tech innovation |
| **Red-chip (already listed overseas)** | ≥ RMB 50 billion | + leading market position & ≥ RMB 3 billion recent year revenue |
| **VIE structure** | Same as applicable standard | VIE agreement disclosure, risks of contract-based control |
| **Dual-class share (差异化表决权)** | ≥ RMB 10 billion **OR** ≥ RMB 5 billion + ≥ RMB 500M revenue | Class-B shares ≤ 10x Class-A voting power; sunset clauses; must pass special resolution |
| **Red-chip without overseas listing** | Same as applicable standard | Additional proof of compliance with PRC law |

## 4. 创业板 (ChiNext / GEM) — "三创四新"

### Required Qualitative Criteria
企业须符合"三创四新"定位：
- **三创**: 创新、创造、创意 (Innovation, Creation, Creativity)
- **四新**: 新技术、新产业、新业态、新模式 (New tech, new industry, new format, new model)

### 12 Negative Industry List (不支持行业)
1. Agriculture (agriculture/forestry/fishery/herding) — except agritech
2. Mining (mining/quarrying)
3. Food & beverage manufacturing (农副食品加工)
4. Textile & apparel
5. Ferrous metal smelting & processing
6. Power & heat generation & supply
7. Construction (construction & decoration)
8. Wholesale & retail trade
9. Transport & logistics
10. Accommodation & catering
11. Financial services (banking/securities/insurance)
12. Real estate

*Note: Enterprises with "三创四新" characteristics serving above industries may still apply.*

### Standard 1: Profit
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Net profit (recent 2 years, both positive) | Cumulative ≥ RMB 50 million |

### Standard 2: Market Cap + Profit + Revenue
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 1 billion |
| Net profit (most recent year, positive) | ≥ RMB 100 million |
| Revenue (most recent year) | ≥ RMB 100 million |

### Standard 3: Market Cap + Revenue
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 5 billion |
| Revenue (most recent year) | ≥ RMB 300 million |

## 5. 北交所 (BSE) — Standards

### Prerequisite
**Must have been listed on NEEQ Innovation Layer (股转系统创新层) for ≥ 12 continuous months.**

### Standard 1: Market Cap + Profit + ROE
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 200 million |
| Net profit (recent 2 years, both positive) | Cumulative ≥ RMB 15 million |
| ROE (recent 2 years, avg) | ≥ 8% |

### Standard 2: Market Cap + Revenue + Revenue Growth
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 400 million |
| Revenue (recent 2 years, avg) | ≥ RMB 100 million |
| Revenue CAGR (recent 3 years) | ≥ 30% |
| Operating cash flow (recent year, positive) | — |

### Standard 3: Market Cap + Revenue + R&D
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 800 million |
| Revenue (most recent year) | ≥ RMB 200 million |
| R&D investment / Revenue (recent 3 years, cumulative) | ≥ 8% |
| *R&D expenditure must be ≥ RMB 15M (recent 3 years cumulative)* | |

### Standard 4: Market Cap Only (Pre-revenue)
| Metric | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Market cap (estimated) | ≥ RMB 1.5 billion |
| R&D investment + patents meet comparable thresholds |
| Target: innovative pharma, advanced devices, etc. |

## 6. Industry Restrictions Summary

| Board | Industry Restrictions |
|---|---|
| **主板** | No restrictive list; "红黄灯" window guidance: some traditional manufacturing disfavored |

| Directors/senior management | 12 months + annual ≤25% | All boards |
