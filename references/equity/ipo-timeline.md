## 1. Timeline Benchmarks by Board

### 主板 (Main Board) — Typical: 16–28 months
| Phase | Min (mo) | Typical (mo) | Max (mo) | Key Activities |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 改制 (Restructuring) | 1 | 2 | 3 | Legal restructuring, audit of assets, ESOP design |
| 辅导 (Sponsor Tutoring) | 3 | 4 | 6 | CSRC branch tutoring, mock exams, governance readiness |
| 申报 (Filing) | 2 | 2.5 | 3 | Due diligence finalization, prospectus drafting, CSRC submission |
| 审核 (Review) | 6 | 9 | 12 | Inquiry rounds (1–3), amended drafts, oral defense |
| 注册 (Registration) | 1 | 1.5 | 2 | CSRC registration review |
| 发行 (Issuance) | 1 | 1.5 | 2 | Roadshow, bookbuilding, subscription, listing |

### 科创板 (STAR Board) — Typical: 10–16 months
| Phase | Min (mo) | Typical (mo) | Max (mo) | Key Activities |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 改制 | 1 | 1.5 | 2 | Restructuring + VIE unwinding (if needed) |
| 辅导 | 3 | 3.5 | 4 | Tutor + innovation attribute assessment |
| 申报 | 1 | 1.5 | 2 | Filing + SSE pre-communication |
| 审核 | 3 | 5 | 6 | SSE inquiry (typically 1–2 rounds, STAR is faster) |
| 注册 | 0.5 | 1 | 1.5 | CSRC registration |
| 发行 | 1 | 1.5 | 2 | Roadshow, pricing |

### 创业板 (ChiNext / GEM) — Typical: 12–20 months
| Phase | Min (mo) | Typical (mo) | Max (mo) | Key Activities |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 改制 | 1 | 2 | 3 | Restructuring + "三创四新" positioning |
| 辅导 | 3 | 4 | 5 | Standard tutor |
| 申报 | 1 | 2 | 3 | Filing + SZSE pre-communication |
| 审核 | 5 | 7 | 8 | SZSE inquiry (1–3 rounds) |
| 注册 | 0.5 | 1 | 1.5 | CSRC registration |
| 发行 | 1 | 1.5 | 2 | Roadshow, pricing |

### 北交所 (BSE) — Typical: 8–12 months
| Phase | Min (mo) | Typical (mo) | Max (mo) | Key Activities |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 改制 | 1 | 1.5 | 2 | Prerequisite: NEEQ Innovation Layer |
| 辅导 | 2 | 2.5 | 3 | BSE tutor (lighter than SSE/SZSE) |
| 申报 | 0.5 | 1 | 1.5 | BSE filing |
| 审核 | 3 | 3.5 | 4 | BSE inquiry (1–2 rounds) |
| 注册 | 0.5 | 0.75 | 1 | BSE registration (faster) |
| 发行 | 0.5 | 1 | 1.5 | Issuance |

## 2. Detailed Phase Breakdown

### Phase 1: Restructuring (改制)
| Sub-Task | Duration (weeks) | Owner | Predecessor |
|---|---|---|---|
| Kick-off meeting | W1 | Sponsor + Company | — |
| Legal DD + audit scope | W1–W4 | Lawyer + Auditor | Kick-off |
| Asset/lability carve-out | W3–W8 | Auditor + Company | Legal DD |
| Financial audit (3Y+CP) | W4–W12 | Auditor | Carve-out |
| Internal control review | W8–W14 | Accountant | Financial audit |
| ESOP design + implementation | W6–W16 | Lawyer + Company | Carve-out |
| Legal restructuring completion | W8–W16 | Lawyer | All above |

### Phase 2: Sponsor Tutoring (辅导)
| Sub-Task | Duration (weeks) | Owner | Predecessor |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tutor registration with CSRC branch | W1–W2 | Sponsor | Restructuring complete |
| Internal training sessions | W2–W8 | Sponsor + Lawyer | Registration |
| Mock board/shareholder meetings | W6–W12 | Sponsor | Training |
| Mock CSRC inquiry | W10–W16 | Sponsor | Training |
| Tutor final report | W16–W18 | Sponsor | All tasks |

### Phase 3: Filing (申报)
| Sub-Task | Duration (weeks) | Owner | Predecessor |
|---|---|---|---|
| Prospectus finalization | W1–W6 | All intermediaries | Tutoring complete |
| Internal intermediary sign-off | W6–W8 | All | Prospectus draft |
| Board/shareholder approval | W8–W10 | Company | Prospectus draft |
| CSRC/exchange filing | W10–W12 | Sponsor | Board approval |

### Phase 4: Review (审核)
| Sub-Task | Duration (weeks) | Owner | Deadline |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-review internal check | W1–W4 | Exchange/CSRC | — |

| Response to R3 | W18–W20 | All intermediaries | ≤1 month |
| Listing committee notice | W20–W22 | Exchange/CSRC | 7 days before hearing |
| Listing committee hearing | W22+ | Committee | — |

### Phase 5: Registration (注册)
| Sub-Task | Duration (weeks) | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Post-hearing filing to CSRC | W1 | Sponsor |
| CSRC registration review | W1–W4 | CSRC |

### Phase 6: Issuance (发行)
| Sub-Task | Duration (weeks) | Owner |
|---|---|---|

| Roadshow + bookbuilding | W3–W5 | Underwriter |

| Allotment + listing | W6–W8 | Underwriter + Exchange |

## 3. Parallel vs Sequential Task Mapping

### Can Run in PARALLEL
| Task Group | Phase | Concurrent Tasks |
|---|---|---|
| Auditor (Financial DD) | Restructuring W4–W12 | Legal DD (Lawyer), Asset carve-out (All) |
| Prospectus Chapters 5 (Business) + 11 (Financial) | Filing W1–W6 | Can draft simultaneously; cross-ref at finalization |
| Use-of-proceeds feasibility study | Filing W1–W6 | Runs parallel to prospectus drafting; separate consultant |
| Investor roadshow preparation | Review W16+ | Can prepare materials while waiting for hearing |

### MUST Run SEQUENTIALLY
| Task A | Task B | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Restructuring complete | Sponsor tutoring start | Legal entity must exist |
| Tutoring complete | CSRC/exchange filing | Tutor report required |
| Filing accepted (受理) | Inquiry rounds | Gatecheck before review |
| All inquiries responded | Listing committee hearing | Complete record |
| Committee approved | Registration filing | Pass first |
| Registration approved (批文) | Roadshow + issuance | Authorized |

## 5. Regulatory Deadlines (Non-extendable)

| Deadline | From Event | Duration | Consequence if Missed |
|---|---|---|---|
| Inquiry response (R1) | Inquiry letter date | ≤3 months | Application may lapse |
| R2 response | R2 inquiry date | ≤2 months | Application may lapse |
| R3 response | R3 inquiry date | ≤1 month | Application may lapse |
| Financial data validity | Audit report date | ≤6 months (extension to 7 months possible) | Must update financials |
| IPO launch | Registration approval | ≤12 months | Approval lapses; must re-apply |
| Pricing | Roadshow bookbuild final | — | Must list within T+N schedule |

---

## 6. Audit Report Freshness Windows

| Scenario | Max Financial Data Age |
|---|---|
| At filing | ≤6 months from most recent audit date |
| During review | ≤7 months (with CSRC/Exchange extension approval for +1 month) |
| At hearing | ≤6 months (often requires updated financials midway) |
| At registration | ≤6 months from updated audit |

**Practical implication:** If audit report is as of Dec 31, must file by Jun 30 (6 months). If review extends past Jun 30, must update to at least Jun 30 interim (半年报). Large IPOs may undergo 2–3 financial updates during the process.
