# Prospectus Section Guide — Detailed Reference

> **Purpose:** Structural reference for prospectus drafting, review preparation, and consistency checking.
> **Last Updated:** 2026-05

---

## Section 1: Cover, Table of Contents, Definitions (封面、目录、释义)

### Positioning
Establishes document legal identity. First impression for regulators.

### Sub-Section Breakdown
- Company name, stock type, par value
- Issuance volume and proposed listing board
- Sponsor (保荐机构) and lead underwriter
- Signature date
- Disclaimer statement block
- Table of contents (auto-generated)
- Definitions table: all technical, legal, and regulatory terms

### Mandatory Content Checklist
- [ ] Full legal name of issuer (Chinese + English)
- [ ] Stock abbreviation and code (if assigned)
- [ ] Sponsor name, legal representative, address, contact
- [ ] Lead underwriter name
- [ ] Legal advisor name (发行人律师)
- [ ] Auditor name (审计机构)
- [ ] Appraiser name (评估机构) — if applicable
- [ ] Statement of director/management responsibility
- [ ] CSRC disclaimer paragraph
- [ ] All defined terms referenced in body must appear

### Common Regulatory Focus
- Sponsor contact info accuracy
- Definition consistency across entire document

### Typical Error Cases
- Anonymized Case P-01-001: Definitions section missing key term used in Risk Factors chapter. CSRC Inquiry Round 1 asked for definitions audit.
- Anonymized Case P-01-002: Sponsor name mismatch between cover and intermediary introduction section.

## Section 2: Overview (概览)

### Positioning
Executive summary of core offering. Many investors only read this section.

### Standard Sub-Sections
1. Issuer profile (company name, date of incorporation, registered capital, address)
2. Issuance overview (stock type, par value, issuance volume, % of post-IPO capital)
3. Use of proceeds summary
4. Financial highlights (last 3 fiscal years + current period)
5. Business model summary (2–3 paragraphs)

### Mandatory Content Checklist
- [ ] Issuance volume (万 shares)
- [ ] Pre-IPO and post-IPO share capital
- [ ] Financial figures MUST match Section 11 (Financial Info) exactly
- [ ] Summary of use of proceeds projects
- [ ] Sponsor recommendation opinion summary

### Common Regulatory Focus
- Financial figures cross-referenced correctly
- Proposed listing board and section correctly stated

### Typical Error Cases
- Anonymized Case P-02-001: Revenue figure in Overview differed from audited financials by RMB 2.3M due to rounding; corrected in Amended Draft #2.
- Anonymized Case P-02-002: Post-IPO shareholding structure misstated — PE stake miscalculated.

## Section 3: Risk Factors (风险因素)

### Positioning
Forward-looking risk disclosure. Must be specific to the company, not generic boilerplate.

### Standard Sub-Sections
1. Industry and market risks (policy, cyclicality, competition)
2. Operational risks (supply chain, technology, talent)
3. Financial risks (receivables, inventory, exchange rates, interest rates)
4. Legal and compliance risks (IP, environmental, litigation)
5. Issuance-specific risks (lockup expiry, dilution)
6. Other risks (controlling shareholder, VIE, related-party)

### Mandatory Content Checklist
- [ ] Risks are company-specific, not generic
- [ ] Quantitative impact estimates where possible
- [ ] Risk severity ordering (most impactful first)
- [ ] Mitigation measures disclosed for each risk
- [ ] Forward-looking statement disclaimer
- [ ] No risk-free or minimal-risk statements

### Common Regulatory Focus
- Whether risks are genuinely specific to the company
- Whether risks contradict business-plan optimism
- Consistency with business chapter disclosures

### Typical Error Cases
- Anonymized Case P-03-001: Risk section contained 14 generic risks with no company-specific data; CSRC required full rewrite.
- Anonymized Case P-03-002: Risk section stated "minimal competition risk" while Business chapter described "intense competitive landscape" — inconsistency flagged.

## Section 4: Issuer Profile (发行人基本情况)

### Positioning
Company legal identity and history. Foundation for independence and control assessment.

### Standard Sub-Sections
1. Basic corporate information
2. Historical evolution (establishment + material changes)
3. Capital changes and major equity transactions
4. Restructuring and reorganization history
5. Shareholders and actual controller
6. Controlling shareholder and actual controller relationships
7. Sponsor / PE / strategic investor relationships
8. Employee incentive plans (ESOP)
9. Material contracts disclosure

### Mandatory Content Checklist
- [ ] Articles of Association date and amendments
- [ ] Complete capital-change chronology
- [ ] Actual controller determination rationale
- [ ] Control stability over past 3 years
- [ ] Shareholder structure diagram
- [ ] ESOP: participants, shares, lockup, pricing
- [ ] PE terms: VAM clauses, special rights, expiry

### Common Regulatory Focus
- Actual controller has not changed in past 3 years
- ESOP compliance with 《上市公司股权激励管理办法》
- PE special rights terminated before filing
- VAM clauses fully terminated (not suspended)

### Cross-Reference Dependencies
- **MUST MATCH**: Shareholders listed here → Legal Opinion shareholding → Audit Report related party → Section 11 financial related-party disclosure
- **MUST MATCH**: ESOP shares → Section 6 (Directors, Supervisors, Management) → Section 11 (share-based compensation expense)

### Typical Error Cases
- Anonymized Case P-04-001: Actual controller held only 18% equity and relied on Concert Party Agreement to reach 34%; CSRC questioned control stability over 5 inquiry rounds.
- Anonymized Case P-04-002: PE's VAM clause stated "suspended upon IPO filing" but not "terminated"; required termination before hearing.
- Anonymized Case P-04-003: Missing disclosure of indirect shareholder holding >5%.

## Section 5: Business and Technology (业务与技术)

### Positioning
Core business narrative. Most heavily scrutinized section. **MUST demonstrate innovation attributes (STAR/GEM).**

### Standard Sub-Sections
1. Primary business and products/services
2. Competitive landscape and market position
3. Sales and procurement model
4. Technology and R&D system
5. Key intangible assets and permits
6. Environmental, safety, and quality certifications
7. Overseas operations (if applicable)

### Mandatory Content Checklist
- [ ] Primary business revenue breakdown by product/region/channel
- [ ] Top 5 customers and % of revenue (each year)
- [ ] Top 5 suppliers and % of procurement (each year)
- [ ] Market share data with sources
- [ ] Competitive advantages vs. key competitors
- [ ] Patent/intangible asset register (number, type, validity)
- [ ] R&D expenditure by year (MUST match audit report footnote)
- [ ] R&D team size, qualifications
- [ ] Core technology descriptions and protection status
- [ ] All permits, licenses, certifications
- [ ] Innovation attribute demonstration (STAR/GEM boards)

### Common Regulatory Focus
- Revenue breakdown MUST MATCH financial analysis in Section 11
- Customer concentration risk assessment (>50% from top 5)
- R&D expenditure vs R&D team employee compensation (consistency)
- Patent ownership (not shared, not licensee-only)
- Environmental compliance (all production bases certified)
- Whether technology truly qualifies for STAR/GEM innovation criteria

### Cross-Reference Dependencies
- **MUST MATCH**: Section 5 product revenue breakdown → Section 11 revenue analysis
- **MUST MATCH**: Section 5 R&D expenditure → Section 11 R&D footnote → Section 4 (innovation attribute)
- **MUST MATCH**: Section 5 related-party sales/purchases → Section 11 related-party transaction note

### Typical Error Cases
- Anonymized Case P-05-001: Business chapter claimed 18% market share with a 2019 industry report; CSRC required updated data.
- Anonymized Case P-05-002: R&D expenditure in Business chapter (RMB 85M) differed from audit report footnote (RMB 78M); corrected after R2 inquiry.
- Anonymized Case P-05-003: "Core technology" patents held by subsidiary not controlled >50%; independence questioned.

## Section 6: Directors, Supervisors, Senior Management and Core Technical Personnel

### Positioning
Leadership qualification and stability assessment.

### Standard Sub-Sections
1. Directors, supervisors, senior management profiles
2. Core technical personnel profiles
3. Director/Senior Management remuneration
4. Emoluments and benefits
5. Shareholding (direct + indirect + ESOP)

### Mandatory Content Checklist
- [ ] Full CV including prior employers for past 5 years
- [ ] No disqualification events (CSRC, exchange blacklist)
- [ ] No concurrent roles creating conflicts
- [ ] Stability over past 2 years (board/seats unchanged reasonably)
- [ ] Related party relationships disclosed
- [ ] Remuneration figures (MUST match Section 11 management compensation)
- [ ] Shareholding reconciliation with Section 4

### Common Regulatory Focus
- Any director/senior management disqualification history
- Concurrent roles in controlling shareholder entity
- Compensation reasonableness and consistency

## Section 8: Use of Proceeds (募集资金运用)

### Positioning
Capital allocation plan. Must demonstrate strategic coherence and feasibility.

### Mandatory Content Checklist
- [ ] Project list with investment amounts
- [ ] Feasibility study summary for each project
- [ ] Project timeline and milestones
- [ ] IRR / payback period estimates
- [ ] Environmental approvals (环评批复)
- [ ] Land use rights / site approvals
- [ ] Project filing/approval documents (项目备案)
- [ ] Match: Fundraising total = SUM(projects) + working capital ≤30% of total
- [ ] Working capital ≤30% of total use
- [ ] Debt repayment NOT allowed (since 2023 window guidance)

### Common Regulatory Focus
- Working capital ≤30% of total funds
- Project feasibility — not speculative, not cyclical
- Environmental compliance

### Typical Error Cases
- Anonymized Case P-08-001: Total use of proceeds = RMB 800M but sum of projects = RMB 820M; discrepancy resolved in amended draft.

## Section 11: Financial and Accounting Information (财务会计信息与管理层分析)

### Positioning
**Most critical section.** Quantitative backbone. External auditor involvement required.

### Standard Sub-Sections
1. Audit opinion and auditor information
2. Financial statements (3 years comparative)
3. Accounting policies and estimates
4. Management Discussion and Analysis (MD&A):
   - Revenue analysis (product/region/channel → MUST MATCH Section 5)
   - Cost and gross margin analysis
   - Period expense analysis (selling, G&A, R&D, finance)
   - Asset quality analysis (receivables, inventory, PPE, intangible, goodwill)
   - Solvency and liquidity analysis
   - Cash flow analysis
   - Capital expenditure analysis
5. Non-recurring items
6. Financial ratios
7. Earnings forecast (optional)
8. Related-party transactions (MUST MATCH Section 4 + 5)
9. Contingent liabilities and commitments
10. Post-balance-sheet events

### Mandatory Content Checklist
- [ ] Unqualified audit opinion
- [ ] 3 full years + current period financial statements
- [ ] Complete accounting policy notes
- [ ] Revenue analysis dimensions MATCH business chapter breakdown
- [ ] Gross margin year-over-year trends explained
- [ ] Receivable aging analysis and bad debt provision
- [ ] Inventory aging and provision
- [ ] Related-party amounts fully reconciled
- [ ] All government subsidies disclosed
- [ ] Non-recurring items reconciliation

### Cross-Reference Dependencies
| Section 11 Item | Must Match Source |
|---|---|
| Revenue by product/region | Section 5 Business |
| R&D expenditure | Section 5 R&D + Section 4 Innovation |
| Related-party transactions | Section 4 Shareholders + Section 5 |
| Management compensation | Section 6 Remuneration |
| Guarantee amounts | Section 11 Footnote + Legal Opinion |
| Litigation provisions | Legal Opinion |
| Use of proceeds | Section 8 |
| Tax rates and holidays | Section 4 + Section 5 |
| Acquisition goodwill | Section 5 Growth Strategy |

### Common Regulatory Focus
- Revenue recognition policies
- Gross margin anomaly (sudden jumps/drops)
- Related-party pricing fairness
- Adequacy of bad debt provision
- Cash conversion cycle lengthening
- Significant government subsidies as % of profit

### Typical Error Cases
- Anonymized Case P-11-001: Revenue recognition changed from point-in-time to over-time mid-cycle without adequate justification; CSRC required restatement.
- Anonymized Case P-11-002: 应收账款 turnover days increased from 60 to 120 days; business chapter still reported "healthy collection"; inconsistency flagged.

```

    │
    ├── Net Profit, Revenue, Assets ──→ Section 2 Overview
    ├── Revenue Breakdown ──────────→ Section 5 Business [MUST MATCH]
    ├── Related Party Amounts ──────→ Section 4 Shareholders
    │                               → Section 5 Related-Party Sales/Purchases
    ├── R&D Expenditure ───────────→ Section 5 R&D
    ├── Management Compensation ──→ Section 6 Remuneration
    ├── ESOP Expense ─────────────→ Section 4 ESOP
    ├── Tax Rates ────────────────→ Section 4 + 5
    ├── PP&E + CIP ───────────────→ Section 8 Use of Proceeds (future capex)
    ├── Goodwill ─────────────────→ Section 5 Acquisitions
    └── Financial Ratios ──────────→ Section 2 Overview

Legal Opinion (法律意见书)
    │
    ├── Shareholder Structure ─────→ Section 4 Actual Controller
    ├── Litigation ───────────────→ Section 3 Risk Factors
    │                               → Section 11 Contingencies
    ├── IP Rights ────────────────→ Section 5 Technology
    ├── Environmental ────────────→ Section 5 Production
    └── M&A Restructuring ────────→ Section 4 History

    │
    └── Asset Valuations ─────────→ Section 4 Capital Increase/Acquisition
```

## Regulatory-Compliant Phrasing Library

### General Disclaimers
| Context | Standard Phrasing |
|---|---|
| Limited assurance | "本公司董事会、监事会及董事、监事、高级管理人员保证..." |
| Responsibility allocation | "保荐机构承诺因其为发行人首次公开发行股票制作、出具的文件有虚假记载..." |
| Forward-looking | "本招股说明书所引用的行业数据、市场预测等信息均来自独立的第三方研究报告..." |

### Risk Disclosure
| Context | Standard Phrasing |
|---|---|
| Competition risk | "随着行业发展，新的竞争者可能进入市场，导致行业竞争加剧，公司市场份额和盈利水平面临下滑风险..." |
| Customer concentration | "发行人对前五大客户的销售占比超过X%，其中对A客户的销售占比超过Y%，存在客户集中度较高的风险..." |

### Financial
| Context | Standard Phrasing |
|---|---|
| Revenue increase explanation | "公司营业收入同比增长主要系X产品销量提升及Y客户订单增加所致..." |
| Gross margin decline | "毛利率下降主要系原材料价格上涨及产品结构调整，低毛利产品收入占比提升共同作用所致..." |

### Use of Proceeds
| Context | Standard Phrasing |
|---|---|
| Project feasibility | "本项目已经X号《投资项目备案证》备案，并已取得Y号《环境影响报告书的批复》..." |

### Independence
| Context | Standard Phrasing |
|---|---|
| Five-separation | "公司在资产、人员、财务、机构、业务等方面完全独立于控股股东和实际控制人..." |
