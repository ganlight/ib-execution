## 2. Private Placement (定向增发 / 非公开发行)

### Key Conditions
| Condition | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Profit requirement** | No hard quantitative profit test |
| **Lockup period** | Controlling shareholder/actual controller/strategic investor: **36 months**; Other investors: **6 months** |
| **Pricing base** | ≥ 80% of average price over 20 trading days prior to pricing base date |
| **Pricing base date** | Board resolution date, shareholder meeting date, or issuance start date |
| **Object limits** | ≤ 35 investors (GEM/STAR) or ≤ 35 (Main Board) |
| **Interval** | ≥ 6 months from previous fundraising (≥ 18 months if prior placement) |
| **Use of proceeds** | Working capital ≤ 30%; no financial investment; no debt repayment (some exceptions) |

### Lockup by Investor Type
| Investor Type | Lockup |
|---|---|
| Controlling shareholder / actual controller | 36 months |
| Controlling shareholder through offshore entity | 36 months |
| Strategic investor (with board approval) | 36 months |
| Directors, supervisors, senior management | 36 months |
| Institutional investors (funds, QFII, etc.) | 6 months |
| Natural persons / retail | 6 months |
| Existing shareholder (non-control) participating | 18 months if ≥5% |

### Pricing Formula
```
Issue Price ≥ (Sum of closing prices over 20 trading days preceding base date) / 20 × 80%
```
Where base date = one of: board resolution date, shareholder meeting date, or issuance start date.

### STAR / GEM Specifics
- Smaller-scale issuance (≤30% of total shares + ≤RMB 300M) enjoys simplified procedure
- Object limit: ≤35 investors
- Pricing: same ≥80% rule

|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2024 | ~210 | ~155 | 0.8–2.5 | ~74% |
| 2025Q1 | ~48 | ~32 | 0.6–2.0 | ~67% |

## 3. Rights Issue (配股)

### Key Conditions
| Condition | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Profit** | Profitable for 3 consecutive fiscal years |
| **ROE** | Weighted-average ROE ≥ 6% (average of recent 3 years) |
| **Rights issue ratio** | ≤ 30% of existing total shares |
| **Shareholder participation** | Controlling shareholder must commit to subscribe in proportion (at least to its %. Under-subscription permitted under certain conditions) |
| **Use of proceeds compliance** | Working capital ≤30% of total |

### Calculation Example
```
Existing shares: 1,000,000,000 shares
Max rights issue: 300,000,000 shares (30%)
Rights issue ratio: 10-for-3
Rights price: determined by sponsor
```

### Recent Statistics (2024–2025)

|---|---|---|---|
| 2024 | ~18 | 30–45% | 1.0–3.0 |
| 2025Q1 | ~4 | 30–40% | 0.8–2.5 |

## 4. Convertible Bonds (可转债)

### Key Conditions
| Condition | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Profit** | Profitable for 3 consecutive fiscal years |
| **ROE** | Weighted-average ROE ≥ 6% (average of recent 3 years) |
| **Bond balance** | ≤ 40% of net assets (end of most recent period) |
| **If guaranteed** | Guarantee meeting requirements |
| **Conversion price** | ≥ average closing price over 20 trading days before prospectus date |
| **Conversion period** | Starts 6 months after issuance |
| **Coupon** | Typically 0.2%–0.5% year 1, increasing ladder to 1.0%–2.0% year 6 |
| **Term** | 6 years |

### Redemption Clause (赎回条款)
Trigger: Stock price ≥ 130% of conversion price for 15 out of 30 consecutive trading days.
Purpose: Force conversion to equity before bond maturity.

### Put Clause (回售条款)
Trigger: Stock price ≤ 70% of conversion price for 30 consecutive trading days.
Purpose: Investor protection — allows bondholders to sell back at par + accrued interest.

### Downward Conversion Price Adjustment (下修条款)
Trigger: Stock price ≤ ~85–90% of conversion price for 15–20 of 30 trading days.
Approval: Shareholder meeting (controlling shareholder must abstain).

|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2024 | ~85 | ~55 | 0.5–3.0 | ~65% |
| 2025Q1 | ~20 | ~12 | 0.5–2.5 | ~60% |

## 5. Public Offering (公开增发)

### Key Conditions
| Condition | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Profit** | Profitable for 3 consecutive fiscal years |
| **ROE** | Weighted-average ROE ≥ 6% (average of recent 3 years) |
| **Dividend** | Cumulative cash dividends ≥ 30% of average distributable profit over recent 3 years |
| **Pricing** | ≥ average closing price over 20 trading days before prospectus date (or previous day) |
| **Use of proceeds** | Working capital ≤30% |

---

## 6. Key Restrictions

### 18-Month / 6-Month Interval Rule
| Previous Fundraising | Type | Next Fundraising Interval |
|---|---|---|
| IPO | — | ≥ 18 months |
| Private placement | Raised >15% of post-fundraising net assets | ≥ 18 months |
| Rights issue / Convertible / Public offering | — | ≥ 6 months |

### Disqualification Events (No Refinancing If...)
- Financial statements with qualified/adverse/disclaimer audit opinion in recent 3 years
- Major violations by controlling shareholder or actual controller
- CSRC administrative penalty pending
- Material litigation with uncertain outcome

## 8. Instrument Selection Decision Tree

```
Can company meet ROE≥6% (3Y avg)?
  ├── NO → Private placement only
  └── YES →
        Urgent need (fast to market)?
          ├── YES → Private placement
          └── NO →
                Large amount (RMB ≥5Bn)?
                  ├── YES → Convertible or combo
                  └── NO →
                        Concerned about dilution?
                          ├── YES → Convertible bond
                          └── NO → Rights issue or public offering
```

## 9. Recent Regulatory Changes (2024–2025)

1. **Nov 2023 CSRC Policy**: Tightened private placement use-of-proceeds — no debt repayment, no financial investment, working capital ≤30%
2. **Feb 2024**: Exchange-level review streamlined for small-scale placements on STAR/GEM
3. **Aug 2024**: Window guidance on sector restrictions — real estate, overcapacity industries restricted
4. **Dec 2024**: "市值管理" guidelines released — affect refinancing timing

## 10. Refinancing vs. IPO Fast Comparison

| Aspect | IPO | Private Placement | Convertible Bond |
|---|---|---|---|
| Minimum profit requirement | Varies by board/standard | No hard requirement | ROE≥6% (3Y) |
| Typical review time | 6–12 months | 3–6 months | 6–10 months |
| Intermediary team | Large team required | Lean | Lean |
| Market timing | Long process, harder to time | Faster, easier to time | Moderate |
| Dilution | Highest (post-IPO all new) | Moderate | Delayed (until conversion) |
| Cost of capital | Lowest (equity) | Moderate | Lowest (initially debt) |
