# Materiality Thresholds — Per Board, Per Product

> **Purpose:** Definitive quantitative threshold reference for disclosure, approval, and compliance triggers across all boards and product types.
> **Last Updated:** 2026-05

---

## 1. Material Contract Disclosure Thresholds

When must a contract/sale/purchase be disclosed?

| Board | Revenue Threshold | Asset/Transaction Threshold | AND Minimum Amount |
|---|---|---|---|
| **主板** | ≥5% of latest audited revenue | ≥5% of latest audited net assets | ≥ RMB 30M |
| **科创板** | ≥10% of latest audited revenue | ≥10% of latest audited net assets | ≥ RMB 10M |
| **创业板** | ≥10% of latest audited revenue | ≥10% of latest audited net assets | ≥ RMB 10M |
| **北交所** | ≥10% of latest audited revenue | ≥10% of latest audited net assets | ≥ RMB 5M |

## 2. Litigation / Arbitration Disclosure Thresholds

When must pending litigation or arbitration be disclosed?

| Board | Amount Threshold | OR Qualitative Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| **主板** | ≥10% of latest audited net assets AND ≥RMB 10M | Any litigation involving controlling shareholder/actual controller (>5%) |
| **科创板** | ≥10% of latest audited net assets AND ≥RMB 10M | Same |
| **创业板** | ≥10% of latest audited net assets AND ≥RMB 10M | Same |
| **北交所** | ≥10% of latest audited net assets AND ≥RMB 10M | Same |

**Disclosure Content:** Case name, court, case number, parties, claims, current status, potential financial impact.

## 7. Product-Specific Materiality

### IPO Prospectus — Materiality for Disclosure

In prospectus, "material" (重大) is not formally defined by CSRC — it's a flexible principle. But the following practical guidance applies:

| Item | Practical Threshold |
|---|---|
| Customer concentration | Single customer >50% of revenue → material risk disclosure |
| Supplier concentration | Single supplier >50% of procurement → material risk |
| Single product dependence | Single product >50% of revenue → material risk |
| Government subsidy dependence | >50% of net profit → material risk |
| Pending litigation | Any that could affect >5% of net assets |
| RPTs | All RPTs during reporting period must be disclosed |
| Gross margin deviation | >5 percentage points from industry average → explain |
| Bad debt | Any single AR >10% of total AR |
| Capital commitment | Any single project >20% of IPO raise |

### M&A — Materiality for Restructuring Report

| Item | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Target financial data error | >5% of any key metric → restate |
| Valuation variance | >10% from appraisal → reassess |
| VAM commitment shortfall | >20% of any year → disclose risk |
| Integration cost surprise | >10% of estimated synergies → revise |

## 8. Calculation Nuances

### "Latest Audited" — Which Period?
- "最近一期经审计" = Most recent audited annual financial statements
- If interim financials have been reviewed (not audited) → still use last audited annual
- Post-balance-sheet events → cumulative threshold may capture post-period transactions

### Cumulation Rule
Transactions with the **same counterparty** or **same transaction type** over a **12-month rolling period** must be cumulated when testing materiality thresholds.

### Net Assets vs Net Assets Attributable to Parent
- Use **net assets attributable to shareholders of the listed company** (归母净资产)
- NOT total net assets including minority interests

## 9. Quick Decision Framework

```
Is the transaction/event amount ≥ threshold?
  ├── YES AND amount ≥RMB floor 
  │     → Board resolution required
  │     → If also ≥5% of relevant metric + RMB 50M → Shareholder meeting required
  └── NO → Business judgment; no mandatory approval
               → But: if qualitatively significant → may still need disclosure

Is there any related party element?
  → Related-party thresholds always apply (these are LOWER than non-RPT thresholds)
  → Guarantees to related parties → ALWAYS shareholder meeting
  → Independent director opinion ALWAYS required for RPTs above board threshold

Is the company in IPO process?
  → Pre-IPO: any event that could affect IPO eligibility → sponsor must be consulted
  → During review: immediate disclosure to exchange + amendment
```
