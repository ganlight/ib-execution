# Related Party Transaction Regulatory Rules — Complete Reference

> **Purpose:** Comprehensive reference for RPT identification, approval, disclosure, and ongoing monitoring.
> **Last Updated:** 2026-05

---

## 1. Related Party Definition

### CSRC / Exchange Definition (per 《上市公司信息披露管理办法》)

#### Natural Person Related Parties (自然人关联方)
| Category | Specific |
|---|---|
| Controlling shareholder (控股股东) | Directly holds >30% **OR** actual control |
| Actual controller (实际控制人) | Ultimate controller through any chain |
| Directors, supervisors, senior management | All board members, supervisors, C-suite |
| Close family members of above | Spouse, parents, children, siblings, parents-in-law |

#### Legal Person Related Parties (法人关联方)
| Category | Specific |
|---|---|
| Controlling shareholder's controlled entities | Any company controlled by controlling shareholder |
| Companies under common control | Sister companies under same actual controller |
| Entities where directors/executives hold key positions | Where DSMs serve as director/senior management in another entity |
| Entities controlled by close family members | Spouse's company, child's company, etc. |
| Entities in which company holds >5% (associate) | Associate entities, joint ventures |
| Substantial shareholder (>5%) | Any shareholder holding >5% (corporate or natural) |
| **Former related parties** | Within 12 months of relationship ending — still treated as RPT |
| **Future related parties** | Parties expected to become related within 12 months based on agreements |

#### Special Cases
| Case | Treatment |
|---|---|
| State-owned enterprises | Two SOEs under same SASAC but separate listed companies — NOT automatically related if independent operation/management/finance |
| University-affiliated companies | Controlled by same university → related |
| PE/VC funds | Same GP manages different funds → likely related |

## 2. Materiality Thresholds for RPT Approval

### Board Approval Thresholds
| Board | Revenue RPT | Asset RPT | Profit RPT |
|---|---|---|---|
| 主板 | ≥0.5% of latest audited revenue **AND** ≥RMB 3M | ≥0.5% of latest audited net assets **AND** ≥RMB 3M | ≥5% of latest audited net profit **AND** ≥RMB 3M |
| 科创板 | ≥1% of latest audited revenue **AND** ≥RMB 3M | ≥1% of latest audited net assets **AND** ≥RMB 3M | ≥10% of latest audited net profit **AND** ≥RMB 1M |
| 创业板 | ≥1% of latest audited revenue **AND** ≥RMB 3M | ≥1% of latest audited net assets **AND** ≥RMB 3M | ≥10% of latest audited net profit **AND** ≥RMB 1M |
| 北交所 | ≥1% of latest audited revenue **AND** ≥RMB 3M | ≥1% of latest audited net assets **AND** ≥RMB 3M | ≥10% of latest audited net profit |

### Shareholder Meeting Approval Thresholds
| All Boards | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Revenue RPT | ≥5% of latest audited revenue **AND** ≥RMB 30M |
| Asset/Transaction | ≥5% of latest audited net assets **AND** ≥RMB 30M |
| Profit RPT | ≥50% of latest audited net profit **AND** ≥RMB 5M |
| Any RPT | Board + Independent Director + Sponsor pre-approval + Independent Auditor opinion if >¥30M |

## 3. Approval Procedures

### Step-by-Step Approval Process

```
1. Identification
   └── Company identifies proposed RPT

2. Preliminary Fairness Assessment
   └── Pricing analysis: market comparison, arms-length test

3. Board Pre-Review
   ├── Independent directors review transaction
   ├── Independent director opinion issued (事前认可意见)
   └── Sponsor opinion (for major RPTs)

4. Board Resolution
   ├── Related directors RECUSE themselves (关联董事回避)
   ├── ≥2/3 of non-related directors approve
   └── If <3 non-related directors → escalate to shareholder meeting

5. Shareholder Meeting (if applicable)
   ├── Related shareholders RECUSE themselves (关联股东回避)
   └── ≥2/3 of non-related shareholders approve

6. Disclosure
   └── Announce within 2 trading days

7. Execution
   └── Subject to ongoing monitoring
```

### Key Rules
- **Recusal (回避)**: Related board members MUST recuse; related shareholders MUST recuse
- **No proxy avoidance**: Cannot circumvent threshold by splitting into smaller transactions
- **12-month cumulation**: RPTs of same type with same counterparty within 12 months are cumulated
- **Accrual**: RPTs expensed over time → full commitment tested at authorization

## 4. Disclosure Requirements

### Immediate Disclosure (within 2 trading days)
| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Transaction counterparty | Full name, relationship to company |
| Transaction type + amount | Purchase/sale/lease/guarantee/loan etc. |
| Pricing principle | Market comparison data |
| Independent director opinion | Published simultaneously |
| Sponsor opinion (if required) | Published simultaneously |
| Impact assessment | Effect on financials and operations |

### Prospectus Disclosure (IPO)
- Full related-party relationship diagram
- Complete transaction register for reporting period (3 years + current period)
- Pricing fairness analysis for each material RPT
- Internal control policy for managing RPTs
- Commitment to reduce and regulate RPTs
- Projection of RPT reduction

## 5. Fair Pricing Documentation

### Pricing Methods (in CSRC-preferred order)
| Method | Documentation Required |
|---|---|
| **Comparable market price** | 3+ comparable third-party transactions; market benchmark data |
| **Cost-plus** | Cost breakdown; % markup justification; industry benchmark markup |
| **Contractual / Negotiated** | Bargaining process; negotiation minutes; why market/comparable unavailable |
| **Appraised value** | Independent certified appraisal report |

### Documentation Checklist for Each Material RPT
- [ ] Transaction agreement
- [ ] Pricing analysis memorandum
- [ ] Independent market data
- [ ] 3 comparable third-party transactions (anonymized)
- [ ] Board meeting notice, agenda, attendance, minutes
- [ ] Independent director pre-approval opinion
- [ ] Board resolution (with recusal note)
- [ ] Shareholder meeting notice and resolution (if applicable)
- [ ] Announcement
- [ ] Sponsor opinion (if applicable)

## 6. Types of RPTs — Special Rules

### Guarantees (关联担保)
- **All** related-party guarantees require shareholder meeting approval (no materiality threshold)
- Guarantees to controlling shareholder/actual controller are generally prohibited post-IPO
- Existing guarantees must be unwound before IPO filing

### Related-Party Fund Transactions (资金往来)
- Loans TO related parties: strictly regulated; consider as "资金占用"
- Loans FROM related parties: must be arms-length, at market interest rates
- Fund pooling / cash management arrangements → independence risk

### Related-Party Asset Acquisitions/Disposals
- Must go through appraisal if material (>50% net assets)
- Appraiser must be independent and qualified
- Pricing must reference appraisal plus market negotiation

### Routine RPTs (日常关联交易)
- Annual estimate approved by shareholders + disclosed
- Quarterly actual vs. estimate comparison
- If actual exceeds estimate → amended approval or explanation
- Examples: ongoing supply/purchase relationships

## 7. Ongoing Transaction Monitoring (Post-IPO / Listed Co.)

| Monitoring Activity | Frequency | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| RPT register maintenance | Continuous | CFO office |
| RPT forecast vs. actual | Quarterly | Finance + Board secretary |
| Threshold monitoring | Per transaction | Finance |
| Pricing review | Annual (routine RPTs) | Independent directors + Auditor |
| Annual RPT report to board | Annual | Board secretary |
| Independent auditor RPT review | Annual audit | Auditor |

## 8. Common Violations & Enforcement

### Red-Flag Patterns
| Pattern | Regulatory Response |
|---|---|
| Undisclosed RPTs discovered in audit | Administrative penalty + rectification order |
| RPT amount exceeding approved limit without amending | Warning letter + fine (RMB 300K–5M) |
| RPT pricing clearly unfair (transfer pricing) | Investigation + penalty + restatement |
| Fund embezzlement through disguised RPTs | CSRC investigation + criminal referral |
| Failure to recuse at board/shareholder meeting | Resolution invalid; administrative penalty |

### Anonymized Enforcement Cases (Recent)
**Case R-01 (2024):** Company A (main board listed) entered into RMB 180M purchase agreement with controlling shareholder's affiliate without shareholder meeting approval. Transaction was 8.5% of net assets (exceeds 5% threshold). CSRC: warning letter, RMB 2M fine, rectification order. Shareholder meeting subsequently approved with recusal; no restatement needed.

**Case R-02 (2024):** Company B (GEM listed) had 3 years of routine supply RPTs with controlling shareholder's private company. Quarterly actuals consistently exceeded approved annual estimates by 30–50% without amendment. CSRC: reprimand, fine, internal control overhaul.

**Case R-03 (2024 pre-IPO):** Company C (STAR filing) had RMB 50M undisclosed loan to controlling shareholder, discovered during exchange review. IPO application withdrawn. Re-filed 12 months later after full repayment + strengthened internal control.

## 9. RPT Internal Control Best Practices

| Practice | Purpose |
|---|---|

| Automatic threshold alert system | Prevents exceeding limits |
| RPT pre-approval workflow | Documented approval chain |
| Quarterly audit committee review | Independent oversight |
| Annual independent auditor RPT review | External verification |
| Annual training for directors/executives | Awareness and compliance |
| RPT disclosure template | Standardized disclosure |
| Arms-length pricing database | Market price justification |
