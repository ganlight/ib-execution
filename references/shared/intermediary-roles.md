> **Purpose:** Definitive reference for intermediary scope, responsibility allocation, and fee negotiation.
> **Last Updated:** 2026-05

## 1. Core Intermediaries Overview

| Role | Title (Chinese) | Primary Function | Required For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sponsor** | 保荐机构 (投行) | Lead arranger; overall project management; filing; sponsor opinion | All IPOs, major refinancing, backdoor |
| **Lead Underwriter** | 主承销商 | IPO marketing, bookbuilding, allocation | All IPOs (usually same as sponsor) |
| **Lawyer** | 发行人律师 | Legal due diligence, legal opinion | All transactions |
| **Accountant** | 审计机构 / 申报会计师 | Financial audit, IC audit, comfort letters | All transactions |
| **Appraiser** | 评估机构 | Asset valuation, appraisal report | M&A (IPO: only if capital contribution with non-cash assets) |
| **Industry Consultant** | 行业顾问 | Market data, competitive analysis | Optional; common in large IPOs |
| **PR Consultant** | 财经公关 | Media management, roadshow support | Optional |

## 4. Intermediary Selection Criteria

| Dimension | Sponsor | Lawyer | Accountant | Appraiser |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **CSRC/Exchange ranking** | A-class or above | — | No ongoing sanctions | Securities-qualified |
| **Industry experience** | ≥3 deals in same sector in recent 3 years | ≥2 IPOs in same sector | Industry specialist partner | Similar-industry appraisal |
| **Track record** | No recent enforcement actions, approval rate | Clean disciplinary record | Clean inspection record | Clean record |
| **Team** | Dedicated MD + VP + Associate + Analyst | Partner + 2+ associates | Partner + 2+ senior managers + team | Lead appraiser + 2+ |
| **Conflicts check** | No conflicting engagements | No conflicts | No self-review threat | No conflicts |

### Ranking References
- Sponsor: CSRC annual classification (A/B/C, A is best)
- Lawyer: 钱伯斯 / Legal 500 ranking for capital markets
- Accountant: Big Four (PwC, Deloitte, EY, KPMG) or top-tier domestic (Baker Tilly China, Pan-China, etc.)
- Appraiser: 中企华, 中联, 天健兴业 — top domestic firms

## 5. Replacement Procedures

### Triggers for Replacement
- Regulatory sanction (CSRC investigation, suspension)
- Conflict of interest (e.g., auditor independence issue)
- Performance failure (missed deadlines, quality issues)
- Relationship breakdown (client-intermediary)

### Process
1. Board resolution to terminate engagement
2. New intermediary engagement agreement
3. New intermediary's confirmation of independence and qualifications
4. Appointment of new intermediary → handover due diligence
5. Disclosure to CSRC/exchange
6. Amended prospectus/legal opinion/audit report filing
7. No automatic restart of review clock if replacement is smooth

### Consequence of Replacement
- Inquiry round may extend
- Additional cost (new intermediary fee + potential re-audit)
- Reputational risk (regulator may question why previous intermediary was replaced)

## 6. Communication Protocols

### Regular Cadence
| Meeting | Frequency | Attendees | Agenda |
|---|---|---|---|
| Weekly project call | Every Monday | All intermediaries + Company | Status updates, blockers, next-week plan |
| Core team call | As needed | Sponsor + Company | Escalation, major decisions |
| Steering committee | Monthly | All partner-level leads + Company CEO/CFO | Progress review, major risks |
| Pre-filing review | Before filing | All | Final document review, sign-off |
| Pre-inquiry-review | Before submitting response | All | Response quality check |
| Mock hearing | 1–2 weeks before hearing | All | Q&A simulation |

### Document Ownership
| Document | Owner | Approvers | Distribution |
|---|---|---|---|
| Prospectus | Sponsor | All intermediaries + Company | CSRC/Exchange |
| Legal opinion | Lawyer | Sponsor review | CSRC/Exchange |
| Audit report | Accountant | Sponsor review | CSRC/Exchange |
| Appraisal report | Appraiser | Sponsor review | CSRC/Exchange |
| DD working papers | Respective intermediaries | — | Internal |
| Inquiry responses | Sponsor (compile) | Respective experts | CSRC/Exchange |

### Escalation Path
```
Issue identified (any party)
    → Raise at weekly call
    → If not resolved → Core team call
    → If major (regulatory, material financial) → Steering committee
    → If deal-threatening → Company CEO + Sponsor MD emergency meeting
```
